    Harmonology: Improve the Quality of your Life

    You create your own reality whether you know it or not. Get into the process of personal transformation! Move to the next level of awareness and get more deeply connected with your loved ones and soul mates. All your healing and desires are here for you.

    My name is Stephen O'Connor. I am a composer, musician and Qi Gong advocate. My lifelong passion is helping others in their journey of personal growth. The premise of my work is that "we are vibrational beings who create our own reality." I am an initiate of the Western Esoteric Tradition and I offer family and individual counseling in interpersonal relationships as well as workshops and classes on Qi Gong.

    If your relationships are feeling like too much of a challenge or you seek to improve the quality of your life and relationships, I offer Harmonology to help you create the life you want and deserve.
