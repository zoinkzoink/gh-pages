    Stephen John O'Connor

    Author of Harmonology and Counterpoint to Reality

    I was born and raised in a small lower class working neighborhood in San Diego. I am the father of two grown children. I love cooking, great wine, friends and living in Mexico. And, after 55 years I still play a pretty good guitar.

    After working in the music business for 40 years and composing for film, TV and radio, I am now pursuing my life-long passion of personal growth in the field of relationships and energy work. I work with individuals, couples and groups and currently teach an ongoing Qi Gong course in Mexico.

    I am an initiate of the Knights Templar, the Rosicrucian Order, C.I.R.C.E.S International, the Martinist Order, the Order of Pythagorus and the Order of Melchizedek, and a committed devotee of the ancient art of Qi Gong.

    I specialize in helping others to understand the mechanics of the nature of their personal vibrational relationships and help them transform those relationships to create the loving and successful lives they want and deserve.
